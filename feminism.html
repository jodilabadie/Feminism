What is the Feminism Movement

Feminism is the movement created to advocated for women's rights on the basis of the equality of the sexes. Contrary to popular beliefs the feminism movement was not created to belittle men or make woman superior. It was simply made to fight for gender equality. For years women have worked the same jobs that men work and have received less pay than men.They dont receive less because they dont work as well men, they receive less simply because they're women.Imagine working the same hours, working just as hard and being just as committed to your job as someone else but receiving less money because society does not respect and they believe that men deserve more than you do. The focus of this movement is to fight to ensure that women and men are seen as equals. To ensure that women are respected the same way men are. To ensure that women receiive the same opportunities as men when they deserve it.

Here are some inequalities faced by women

  1. Unequal pay
  2. Women are less likely to get job opportunities
  3. Women are more likely to experience domestic violence
  4. Men are more likely to be CEO's and see higher rates of promotion
  5. Women are often harassed in the workplace by their superiors or coworkers
  6. Men are held at higher standards than women
  7. Women are more likely to be sexualized

Why YOU should support the movement

If you wish for equal opportunities for your daughters. For our daughters to be raised in a society that views women and men as equals. By supporting the movement you are being a part of a change that we so desperately need. Be on the right side of history.
